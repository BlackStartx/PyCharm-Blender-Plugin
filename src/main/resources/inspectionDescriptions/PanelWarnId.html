Highlight for the two warnings for Panels:

Warning: 'NAME' does not contain '_PT_' with prefix and suffix.
Warning: 'NAME' doesn't have upper case alphanumeric prefix.

Following the naming convention from the official API.

This aim to help the user find the cause of two possible warning that may be encountered when installing the addon.


Prefix and Suffix: posted in Blender.StackExchange.
Alpha-Numeric Prefix: posted in Blender.StackExchange.
